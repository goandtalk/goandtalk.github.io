Finding Toolbars

Global Toolbar

  • At the left side of the screen.
  • Manage global settings.
  • Apply to the current page, or all pages.
  • Hide all section tools.
  • Hide itself.

Section Toolbar

  • At the top of each section.
  • Manage current section.
  • Apply to current section.
  • Turn app on and off.
  • Toggle edit mode.
  • Toggle global toolbar.

Context Toolbar

  • Available in some sections.
  • Toggle button to switch toolbars.
  • Toggle on to edit.
  • Toggle off when done.

Where are Data and Images Imported To

A Database in Your Browser

  • IndexedDB by name
  • Storage space is large enough for texts
  • Limited in space for images, especially on mobile devices
  • Online image service recommended

Image Sources

Online

  • open and public
  • use as background
  • non critical for website message
  • link to images already on the internet, for example Unsplash.
  • see the results and make changes without worrying about storage space
  • free and high quality photos are available
  • resize on demand

Local

  • use for foreground highlight
  • internal, local use
  • store in a specified images folder, to use with exported web page later.
  • when imported, image is displayed in the current session, but not stored in the database. It is already on your disk anyway.

Exercise

Customize Images

  • hide global toolbar to make room for this exercise.
  • find the toolbar of this section, and click on the toggle icon to turn the app on.
  • click on the cog icon to open the options window.
  • go to background image tab, and enter the URL to the image you want to use as background image.
  • if the image is available, you will see your image displayed under this section.
  • click on Edit button to switch to edit mode.
  • click on the image at the side of this section to display a popup window. Choose a image from your local disk.
  • You will see your selected image at the side of this section if the operation is successful.
  • Click on the Editing button to switch off editing mode.
